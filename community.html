Community Service

Her community-engaged work has been recognized with awards from the American Political Science Association, the Association of Professional Schools of International Affairs, and the University of Pittsburgh.

Est. 2021

The Afghanistan Project

Founded after the 2021 collapse of the Afghan republic to bring at-risk Afghan scholars to safety and sustain a community of Afghan scholarship in exile — providing fellowships, research support, and a scholarly home for displaced academics.

2021–2022

Afghan Asylum Assistance Task Force

Coordinating pro bono asylum assistance for Afghans evacuated after the fall of Kabul — connecting evacuees with legal support, expert declarations, and resettlement resources.

2022

Support of Ukrainian Scholars

Emergency support for scholars in Ukraine following the 2022 Russian invasion — sustaining their research and connecting them to the international academic community.
